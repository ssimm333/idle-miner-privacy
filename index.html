Idle Miner – Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Idle Miner ("the game") is a free-to-play idle game developed by an independent developer. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. All game progress (balance, upgrades, achievements) is saved locally on your device only using Android's SharedPreferences storage.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The game displays rewarded video ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalised ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising in your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment information. Google Play's terms apply.

Notifications

The game may send local notifications to remind you of offline earnings. These notifications are generated on-device and no data is transmitted to any server.

Children

The game is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
